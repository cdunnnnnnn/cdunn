Modern Healthcare A mobile-first, responsive editorial site

Modern Healthcare - A mobile-first, responsive editorial site http://cdunn.io/assets/img/posts/20150805-01.jpg
by @cdunnnnnnn
Aug 05, 2015  
 Work  

I started at Crain developing products for Modern Healthcare out of the Chicago office. We spent the majority of 2014 researching & redesigning the site. In the prior year, editorial had done the same for the print magazine and so the same process was applied to the website. Our main goal was to make it responsive and provide our readers with optimized content on any device. We developed it to be mobile-first and used a number of different tools, some of which include Gulp.js, LESS and Pure CSS. After about a month of development, we launched on October 31st.
